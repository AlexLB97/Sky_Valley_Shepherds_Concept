Our Mission

Here at Sky Valley Shepherds we are dedicated to highlighting the best traits of the German Shepherd breed through high quality training and selective breeding practices.

Our dogs come from decorated lines, and our goal is to produce dogs that are driven to work with stable nerves and temperaments.
